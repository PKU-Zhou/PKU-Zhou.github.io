## About me

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at the School of Integrated Circuits, Peking University, advised by Professor [Yufei Ma](http://scholar.pku.edu.cn/yufei.ma).

I'm now working on taping out a chip for Diffusion-Policy Online RL.

And I'm going to attend DAC'26 at Long Beach in CA. in this summer.
